## 1. QUALITY POLICY & OBJECTIVES

### 1.1 Policy Statement
*The project shall be designed and constructed in accordance with the contract specifications, applicable codes and standards, and the client's brief, using a Quality Management System conforming to ISO 9001:2015.*

### 1.2 Quality Objectives
| Objective | Target |
|---|---|
| Works handed over defect-free | < 5 minor defects per zone |
| NCRs closed within 30 days | ≥ 95% |
| ITP holds passed first time | ≥ 90% |
| Client snag list at Practical Completion | < 20 items |
| Final account variation < | 2% over contract sum |

## 4. QUALITY CONTROL ACTIVITIES

### 4.1 Design Quality
- Independent design reviews at RIBA 2, 3, 4
- BIM clash detection (Solibri / Navisworks) — biweekly
- Design check sheets per discipline
- Designer's sign-off before issue for construction

### 4.2 Construction Quality
- ITP for every significant work package (`/05.../02_ITPs/`)
- Pre-pour / pre-close-up checks (mandatory hold points)
- Material inspections at delivery
- Workmanship sample panels for finishes
- Benchmark rooms / areas — approved reference

### 4.3 Testing & Commissioning
- Concrete cube testing every 50m³
- Welding procedures qualified; NDT per spec
- MEP systems pressure-tested
- Integrated Systems Test before handover
