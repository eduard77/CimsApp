## 1. PURPOSE

Define how the project schedule is developed, maintained, monitored, and controlled — aligned to PMBOK Planning domain and NEC/JCT programme requirements.

---

## 2. SCHEDULING METHODOLOGY

### 2.1 Approach
- **Method:** Critical Path Method (CPM)
- **Technique:** Precedence Diagramming Method (PDM)
- **Tool:** ☐ Primavera P6  ☐ MS Project  ☐ Asta Powerproject  ☐ Other: ___

### 2.2 Units of Measure
- Duration unit: **working days** (5-day week unless otherwise specified)
- Calendar: project-specific calendar reflecting holidays, weather windows, and shutdowns

### 2.3 Level of Detail
| Level | Description | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Master milestone schedule | PM |
| 2 | Summary schedule (control accounts) | PM |
| 3 | Baseline detailed schedule | Planner |
| 4 | Short-term look-ahead (6 weeks) | Site Mgr |
| 5 | Daily programme | Foreman |

---

## 3. SCHEDULE BASELINE

### 3.1 Baseline Rules
- Baseline set at **contract award** and approved by Sponsor
- **No retrospective changes** — only approved Change Requests can modify baseline
- Baseline comparison (actual vs planned) reported monthly

### 3.2 Activity Coding
Every activity is coded with:
- **WBS code** (e.g. 1.5.2)
- **Discipline code** (A/S/C/M/E/P)
- **Phase** (Design / Proc / Construction / Commissioning)
- **Responsible party**
- **Area / Zone**

## 5. FLOAT & CRITICAL PATH

- **Total float** owned by the project — not individual activities
- **Free float** may be used by the responsible party
- **Critical path** reviewed at every schedule update
- **Near-critical** activities (< 10 days float) flagged for monitoring

---

## 6. PROGRESS UPDATE CYCLE

| Activity | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Progress update from site | Weekly | Site Mgr |
| Schedule update in tool | Weekly | Planner |
| Baseline comparison | Monthly | Planner |
| Look-ahead issued | Weekly (6-week) | Planner |
| Schedule risk analysis | Quarterly | Risk Mgr + Planner |

---

## 7. DELAY & DISRUPTION MANAGEMENT

### 7.1 Early Warning Notice (EWN)
Any event likely to cause delay → EWN issued within 2 working days → recorded in `/08_Risk_Management/06_Early_Warning_Notices/`.

### 7.2 Compensation Events / EOT Claims
Follows contract terms. Claim package prepared in `/14_Construction_Claims/04_Extensions_of_Time/`.

### 7.3 Recovery Plans
Triggered when SPI < 0.95. Plan to bring project back to baseline within [X] weeks.

---

## 8. PERFORMANCE REPORTING

Monthly schedule report includes:
- Programme percentage complete
- SPI (from EVM)
- Critical path narrative
- Milestone dashboard (planned vs forecast)
- Late activities > 5 days
- Float consumption trend
- Top 10 delays / disruptions this period
- Recovery actions and their impact
