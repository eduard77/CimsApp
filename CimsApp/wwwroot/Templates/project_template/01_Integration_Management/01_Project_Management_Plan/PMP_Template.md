## 1. INTRODUCTION

The Project Management Plan (PMP) integrates all subsidiary management plans and baselines. It is the master plan governing how the project is executed, monitored, controlled, and closed.

### 1.1 Governing Standards
- PMBOK 7th Edition
- PMBOK Construction Extension
- ISO 19650-2 (CDE & information management)
- ISO 9001 (quality)
- ISO 31000 (risk)
- CDM 2015 (health & safety)

### 1.2 Document Control
This document is version-controlled in the CIMS CDE. All revisions must be issued via the change control process.
