# COMMISSIONING MANAGEMENT PLAN

**Document Reference:** [PRJ-CODE]-COM-001
**Revision:** P01
**CDE Status:** Shared · Suitability: S3

---

## 1. PURPOSE

Defines the systematic process for planning, executing, and verifying that building systems are installed, tested, and operating in accordance with the design intent and client's Operational Requirements.

---

## 2. COMMISSIONING PHASES

| Phase | Description | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| **Design Commissioning** | OPR, BOD, commissioning specifications | RIBA 2–4 |
| **Construction Commissioning** | Installation checks, pre-functional, start-up | RIBA 5 |
| **Acceptance Commissioning** | Functional testing, integrated systems test | Late RIBA 5 |
| **Handover Commissioning** | Training, O&M, seasonal commissioning | RIBA 6 |
| **Post-Occupancy** | Seasonal reviews, warranty close-out | RIBA 7 |

---

## 3. SYSTEMS TO BE COMMISSIONED

| System | Responsible | Method | Success Criteria |
|---|---|---|---|
| HVAC — Heating | MEP Contractor | Balance + witness | ± 10% of design flows |
| HVAC — Cooling | MEP Contractor | Balance + witness | ± 10% of design flows |
| Ventilation | MEP Contractor | Balance + witness | Per spec & regs |
| Electrical | Electrical Contractor | Test sheets per BS 7671 | All circuits pass |
| Fire Alarm | Specialist | L1 test | Zero faults |
| Sprinkler | Specialist | Pressure test + flow | Per LPC rules |
| BMS | Specialist | Point-to-point | 100% points verified |
| Lifts | Specialist | Statutory test | LOLER + inspection |
| Security / CCTV | Specialist | Commissioning plan | All cameras/doors tested |
| Public Health | Plumbing | Pressure + flow test | Pass |

## 7. TRAINING

- Client FM team training on all systems
- Recorded video walkthroughs (retained 10 years)
- O&M reading sessions
- Emergency procedures drill

---

## 8. HANDOVER ACCEPTANCE

Systems accepted when:
- All test certificates issued
- O&M manuals complete
- Training delivered & acknowledged
- Commissioning report signed
- Spares register delivered
