## 5. OPERATIONAL CONTROLS

### 5.1 Site Setup
- Bunded fuel storage (110% bund)
- Spill kits at all fuel/chemical storage
- Wheel wash at main site exit
- Hoarding with acoustic lining where needed
- Dust suppression — static + mobile

### 5.2 Waste Management
- Skips: mixed / wood / metal / plasterboard / inert / hazardous
- Licensed waste carriers only
- Waste transfer notes retained 2+ years
- Monthly waste KPI reporting

### 5.3 Water
- Silt management — settlement tank before discharge
- No discharge to watercourse without consent
- Water used on site metered

### 5.4 Noise & Vibration
- Working hours: [per planning conditions]
- Noisy activities scheduled away from sensitive times
- Monitoring at boundary with public / sensitive receptors

### 5.5 Ecology
- Pre-construction ecological survey
- Reasonable Avoidance Measures (RAMs) per survey
- Toolbox talks on protected species

## 7. EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS

| Scenario | Response |
|---|---|
| Spill (fuel / chemical) | Stop source → contain → notify → clean up → report |
| Discovery of contamination | Stop work → isolate → specialist assessment |
| Discovery of archaeology | Stop work → notify curator |
| Flood / extreme weather | Secure site → personnel safety → resume when safe |
